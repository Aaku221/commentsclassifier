The Current Scenario

20 million tweets sent out each day. A billion Facebook posts to monitor. Above all this, fake accounts make it almost impossible to track the offender. Social media has become the go-to platform for abuse. Race, religion, gender and literally every part of the human society is targeted and hurled abuse at. Stalkers, rants, banter, abuse, racist comments - people have had enough of it.

If you find an abusive or racist comment online, targeted towards you or is generally speaking abusive, the current mechanism asks you to manually report it to the respective social media community teams. It turns out that these community teams are bombarded with requests each day and monitoring each one of them is a tedious task. Moreover, some of the requests made by users seem like a mountain made out of a mole hill.

Fake accounts - Spineless people sitting behind computer screens, hurling abuse at the innocent. Even if you manage to report an abusive comment and lets say it gets removed and the user is banned. This fake account guy can always create another "fake account".

What we offer - Our Mission

Manners maketh man. Technology drives the world and it is technology which is the solution. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is the present and the future. This doesn't mean you would have to encounter a Terminator style - Judgement Day anytime soon!

Enter Prospect. Prospect is a machine learning model that makes online conversations better. Prospect has already been trained with abusive, racist and all sorts of lame comments, so it knows how ugly conversations can become. It automatically detects these kind of content and in a split second notifies the admin of it, who then necessary action.

Once Prospect has notified the admin of abusive or racist content, the admin can then take a three-level action - ban the user entirely, remove the abusive content or warn the user. Keep posting abusive content and we will keep deleting it. Only a matter of time before they give up.
